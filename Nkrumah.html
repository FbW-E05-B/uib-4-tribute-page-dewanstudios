Dr Kwame Nkrumah

From Person To Become A President

“The forces that unite us are intrinsic and greater than the superimposed influences that keep us apart.”

  1. 21 September 1909 - Nkroful, Gold Coast (now Ghana)
  2. 1 July 1960 – 24 February 1966 -1st President of Ghana
  3. 21 October 1965 – 24 February 1966 -3rd Chairperson of the Organisation of African Unity
  4. 6 March 1957 – 1 July 1960 -1st Prime Minister of Ghana
  5. 21 March 1952 – 6 March 1957 -1st Prime Minister of the Gold Coast

“I am not African because I was born in Africa but because Africa was born in me.” “The forces that unite us are intrinsic and greater than the superimposed influences that keep us apart.”

-Dr. Kwame Nkrumah
